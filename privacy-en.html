Privacy Policy

App: Anycade · Last updated: June 2026 · Deutsch

Anycade is an emulator and launcher app for classic handheld games (Game Boy, Game Boy Color, Game Boy Advance). The app is built around data minimisation: we operate no servers of our own and do not collect any personal data for advertising, profiling or analytics purposes. This policy informs you in line with the Swiss Data Protection Act (revDSG) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Controller within the meaning of Art. 5 lit. j revDSG / Art. 4 No. 7 GDPR.

2. What data is processed

2.1 Locally on your device

The following data stays solely on your device and is transmitted neither to us nor to third parties:

  • Your game files (ROMs), which you add yourself from your own folders
  • App settings, favourites and save states
  • Cached cover images

Access to your ROM folders uses the Android file picker (Storage Access Framework). The app does not request any broad storage permission.

2.2 Loading cover images (disclosure abroad)

To display cover art, the app loads images from the public service LibRetro Thumbnails (thumbnails.libretro.com). The following is transmitted to that service:

  • your IP address (technically required for any internet request)
  • the name of the respective game (as part of the image address)

The service is operated by a third party (the LibRetro project); its servers may be located outside Switzerland and the EU (e.g. in the USA). This therefore constitutes a disclosure of personal data abroad (Art. 16 f. revDSG / Art. 44 ff. GDPR). An equivalent level of data protection may not exist in those countries. We have no influence over the data processing carried out by LibRetro; their own privacy practices apply.

Purpose: displaying matching cover images for your games. Legal basis (where GDPR applies): our legitimate interest in a functional, appealing presentation (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

How to avoid this transfer: without an active internet connection the app loads no covers; it continues to work with placeholder graphics.

2.3 Purchases

If paid features are offered, payment is processed via Google Play. Payment data is handled by Google; we receive no credit-card or bank details. Google's privacy policy applies.

2.4 No further data collection

  • No advertising, no tracking, no analytics SDKs, no cookies
  • No user accounts and no login (in the current version)
  • No crash reports or usage data are sent to us

3. App permissions

  • Internet – solely to load cover images (see 2.2)

4. Retention period

The data listed under 2.1 stays on your device until you delete it yourself or uninstall the app. We do not store any data about you. Which log data LibRetro keeps when serving images is beyond our knowledge and control.

5. Your rights

Under the revDSG / GDPR you have, in particular, the rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, objection and data portability. As we store no data about you, these rights primarily concern the local data on your device, which you can delete yourself at any time (game library, settings, app uninstall).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority:

  • Switzerland: Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC/EDÖB)
  • EU: the supervisory authority responsible for you

For any data-protection matters, contact us at k.baehler@proton.me.

6. Children's data

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

As the app evolves (e.g. additional emulators, optional cloud sync or user accounts), this privacy policy will be updated accordingly. The version stated above applies.
